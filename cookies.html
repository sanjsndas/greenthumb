Our Cookie Policy Explained

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device as you browse the web. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to site owners. Cookies allow websites to remember your preferences, login details, and other information to improve your user experience. They can be either session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (remain until they expire or you delete them). Cookies don't typically contain personal information but may track how you use a site. Modern web browsers allow you to control cookie settings through their privacy options.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies on our website: Strictly Necessary cookies essential for site functionality, Performance cookies that collect anonymous data about site usage, Functionality cookies that remember your preferences, and Targeting/Advertising cookies that deliver relevant content. Each type serves a specific purpose to enhance your experience. We classify cookies as first-party (set by us) or third-party (set by services we use). Our cookie declaration provides detailed information about each cookie's purpose, duration, and provider. You can manage your cookie preferences through our consent tool.

3. Why We Use Cookies

Cookies help us provide you with a better website experience by enabling basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas. They allow us to understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve performance and content. Cookies remember your preferences to personalize your experience and make the site more relevant to you. They help our marketing efforts by measuring the effectiveness of campaigns and delivering targeted content. Without cookies, some website features may not function properly. We never use cookies to collect sensitive personal information without your consent.

4. Managing Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in various ways through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies from specific sites. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience. Our cookie consent banner allows you to accept or reject different cookie categories. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer. We provide clear information about each cookie category to help you make informed choices. For comprehensive guidance, consult your browser's help documentation on cookie management.

5. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for our website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. These cookies don't store personally identifiable information but are required for features like shopping carts or secure login. Without these cookies, services you've asked for cannot be provided. We don't require consent for essential cookies under most privacy regulations. Examples include cookies that maintain your session during navigation or balance load on our servers. These cookies are typically deleted when you close your browser.

6. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information. This data includes pages visited, time spent on site, and error messages encountered. We use this information to improve how our website works and measure the effectiveness of our content. These cookies don't collect information that identifies visitors personally. The data is aggregated and therefore anonymous. Examples include Google Analytics cookies that help us analyze site traffic patterns. You can opt out of performance cookies without affecting site functionality.

7. Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies allow our website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, more personal features. They may remember your username, language preference, or region to save you time on subsequent visits. These cookies can also be used to remember changes you've made to text size, fonts, and other customizable elements. The information collected is anonymized and doesn't track your browsing activity on other websites. While not strictly necessary, they significantly improve your browsing experience. You can block these cookies but some site features may not work as intended.

8. Targeting/Advertising Cookies

Targeting cookies are used to deliver ads more relevant to you and your interests. They remember that you've visited our website and may share this information with other organizations such as advertisers. These cookies are also used to limit how many times you see an ad and help measure advertising campaign effectiveness. While we use these cookies responsibly, you may prefer to disable them if you're concerned about privacy. These cookies are typically placed by third-party advertising networks with our permission. You can manage these cookies through our consent tool or your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Cookies

We use some third-party services that may set their own cookies to provide features like social media integration, videos, or analytics. These cookies are controlled by the respective third parties and subject to their privacy policies. Examples include cookies from Facebook, YouTube, or Google Maps. We carefully select third-party providers who respect user privacy and comply with data protection laws. However, we can't control how these third parties use cookies once they're set. We provide information about all third-party cookies in our cookie declaration for transparency.

10. Cookie Duration

Cookies have varying lifespans depending on their purpose. Session cookies expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain for a set period (from days to years). We set reasonable expiration periods based on each cookie's function. Essential cookies typically have shorter durations, while analytics cookies may persist longer to track trends. You can see the exact duration of each cookie in our detailed cookie list. Even persistent cookies can be manually deleted through your browser settings at any time. We regularly review cookie durations to ensure they're appropriate for their purpose.

11. GDPR Compliance

Our cookie practices comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) requirements. We provide clear information about cookie usage and obtain consent for non-essential cookies. Our cookie consent mechanism allows granular control over different cookie categories. We maintain records of consent and make it easy to withdraw consent at any time. Data collected through cookies is processed according to our Privacy Policy. We've implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data collected via cookies. EU residents have additional rights regarding their data as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

While we take precautions, users should also practice good security habits like keeping browsers updated and avoiding public computers for sensitive activities. Report any security concerns to our Data Protection Officer immediately.
