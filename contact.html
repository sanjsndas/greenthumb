Comprehensive Contact Information

1. Office Hours

Our main office is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and major holidays. For emergency services outside these hours, please call our after-hours line at (123) 456-7891. Our customer service representatives are available during business hours to answer questions, schedule consultations, and provide estimates. Extended hours may be available during peak seasons.

2. Physical Location

Our headquarters is located at 721 Broadway, New York, NY 10003, USA. The office features a small demonstration garden showcasing our work. Visitors are welcome during business hours, but appointments are recommended for detailed consultations. Parking is available in the adjacent lot, and our facility is wheelchair accessible. Please check in with reception upon arrival. For deliveries, please use the loading dock at the rear of the building.

3. Phone Support

For immediate assistance, call our main line at (123) 456-7890. Our automated system can direct you to the appropriate department: press 1 for new services, 2 for existing customers, 3 for billing, and 4 for all other inquiries. Average wait times are less than 3 minutes during business hours. International customers can reach us at +1-123-456-7890. For hearing-impaired customers, we offer TTY services at (123) 456-7892.

4. Email Communication

Send general inquiries to info@greenthumb.com, service requests to service@greenthumb.com, and billing questions to billing@greenthumb.com. We strive to respond to all emails within 24 business hours. For faster service, please include your account number (if applicable) and a detailed description of your request. Attachments should not exceed 10MB. To ensure delivery, add our domain to your email whitelist and check your spam folder if you don't receive a response.

5. Emergency Services

For urgent matters requiring immediate attention (such as storm damage or hazardous tree situations), call our 24/7 emergency line at (123) 456-7899. This line is monitored continuously by our response team. Additional charges may apply for after-hours services. Describe the situation clearly and provide your exact location. If possible, send photos of the issue to emergency@greenthumb.com to help us prepare the appropriate response team.

6. Social Media

Connect with us on Facebook (@GreenThumbGardens), Instagram (@GreenThumbOfficial), and Twitter (@GT_Gardens). We monitor these platforms daily and typically respond within a few hours. Social media is ideal for general questions, sharing photos of your garden, or seeing examples of our work. For account-specific issues, please contact us through official channels to protect your privacy. We post seasonal tips, promotions, and event announcements regularly.

7. Consultation Appointments

Initial consultations can be scheduled online through our booking system or by calling our office. Most consultations last 60-90 minutes and include a property assessment, discussion of your goals, and preliminary recommendations. There is a $75 consultation fee credited toward any services booked within 30 days. Please have property surveys or plans available if possible. Evening and weekend appointments may be available for an additional fee.

8. Service Area

We serve all five boroughs of New York City and surrounding counties within a 50-mile radius of our headquarters. Service availability may vary by location and season. Some specialized services have narrower geographic limits. Contact us with your ZIP code to confirm coverage in your area. We're expanding our service areas annually and maintain a waiting list for locations we don't currently serve. Corporate accounts may qualify for extended service areas.

9. Billing Inquiries

For questions about invoices, payments, or account balances, contact our billing department at billing@greenthumb.com or (123) 456-7893. Please have your invoice number ready when calling. Payments can be made online through our customer portal, by phone, by mail, or in person at our office. We accept all major credit cards, checks, and electronic transfers. Late payments may incur a 1.5% monthly finance charge. Payment plans may be available for large projects.

10. Employment Opportunities

We're always looking for talented horticulturists, designers, and maintenance professionals. Current openings are posted on our careers page at careers.greenthumb.com. Applications can be submitted online or in person at our HR office (enter through the west entrance). Seasonal positions typically open in March and September. All applicants must pass a background check and drug screening. Benefits include health insurance for full-time employees, training programs, and equipment allowances.

11. Vendor Inquiries

Suppliers and service providers interested in working with GreenThumb Gardens should contact procurement@greenthumb.com. Include your company overview, product/service details, and references. Approved vendors will be added to our bidding list and must complete our vendor certification process, which includes insurance verification and quality standards review.

12. Media Requests
